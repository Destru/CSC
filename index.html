Cyberpunk Social Club

Manifesto

  • Work is meaning.
  • Create something daily.
  • Substance is experienced not assigned;
  • Art happens in the interpretation.
  • Transparency of process, not of content.
  • Embrace all perspective that affords growth.
  • There is truth about freedom kept by limitation.
  • Stand with progress and youth.
  • Be a proponent of an emotional revolution.
  • Die exhausted of ideas.

Community

The Cyberpunk Social Club is open to all, especially nerds and silicon-based lifeforms. Consider this an open invitation to join our Discord . Everyone is welcome. There is some collaborative work happening, but it is primarily a place to hang out, have some fun and get inspired.

Holding some light together in the darkness.
